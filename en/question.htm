Distance HD Rescue Process:

1.If a client' HD suffers from logical fault, don't operate on the HD without destination and limit, or system data would be further destroyed and it would be more difficult to rescue the HD, or even unrecoverable. To remind customers: first, don't conduct any reading on fault HD. Second, if system automatically starts HD rescue procedure when operating system started, terminate computer operation immediately to guarantee the initially complete state of the fault HD finally at the best condition.

 

2.If you decide that we conduct distance HD rescue for you, you can sign into our website to download HD Rescue Form. Click "download and fill in repair forms" on the left bottom of homepage, and click "HD data rescue" to down load HD Rescue Form in your computer. After filling it, you shall send it to our email address as email attachment. You can also contact us by phone. After we have received your repair application form, we will send HD Rescue Form and relevant data rescue procedure to your email address.

 

3.After analyzing your HD Rescue Form, our technician will send the result to your email address in time. Meanwhile, relevant tools and detailed operations will be explained in email.

 

4. Customers shall inform us after having set according to the operations we provide. Our technician will immediately conduct relevant data rescue procedure by establishing remote controlled environment with your computer system. And repair your HD by use of our data rescue tools. To remind you that during the whole process of operation, your computer must keep on-line, or our work cannot proceed once remote controlled environment is cut off. It may take a long time, depending on your HD size, operating system environment and network speed.

 

5. After rescuing your HD data, our technician will give you a call, then you can power off your computer.

 

6. After having rescue data, our technician will send detailed data rescue results to your email address. You can check the results, without error, you shall transfer the service price into our specified bank account and give us a reply by phone.

 

7. After confirming the remittance, our technician will backup your data. If the data is recovered directly on your HD, remote controlled environment will be established again for backup.

 

8. After data recovery completed, we will delete the data on our simulation HD. We guarantee the secrecy of your data and no third party could check them.

 

9.Distance HD Rescue service interface includes accidentally erasing, formatting, partition, ghos or other logical data loss of(DE, SCSI, S-ATA interface), (Mobile HD, USB flash disk, MP3) (CF, SD, Xd, MMC, SM, SMC）（CD-R、CD-R/W）

 

10.Unless the data rescue is very urgent with a small quantity of data, this method is not recommended. The data risk of network samba service would increase relatively in terms of service speed and time. Therefore, customers are recommended to make an appropriate packaging of the storage facilities and send it to our center for data rescue.
